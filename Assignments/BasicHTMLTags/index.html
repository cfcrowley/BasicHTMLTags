Lets Learn about the creators of IHYD

IHYD is made up of 2 guys named Joe Johnson and Michael Lynch. They showcase powerfule Magic the Gathering decks each week in games of commander

The goal of each week is to make 3 of the 4 players salty as one deck goes off. Typically somene will combo off and the other's will have been too scared to interact with them

Elements of a Commander Deck

  • 100 cards

  • has one commander that determines deck color
  • Best to make it as salty as posible
  • Blue is the worst color
